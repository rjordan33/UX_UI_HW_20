Website Redesign

UXUI

Completely overhauled and updated the Hollyhill Hummingbird Farm non-profit website.
This website received a facelift while also enabling visitors the opportunity to schedule a tour through the website.

DATE

Aug 2022 - Sep 2022

TEAM

Rod Jordan, Zeke Wolfe, James Moore

ROLE

User Research / UX/UI Design



Challenge ——

Hollyhill Hummingbird Farm, a 501 Nonprofit corporation, is an educational farm located in Santa Rosa, California. Its focus is on biodynamics, sustainability, and the ethical treatment of life. The farm was founded in 2011 by an aerospace engineer. The website appears to have been around since about that same time.


Redesign website to feature donations and tours to bring revenue to the website. Increase the web presence of the non-profit organization by updating website to 2022 standards.
